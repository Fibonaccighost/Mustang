Mustang

Ride with style

Drift with attitude

  1. Powerful Engine: The Mustang GT is known for its high-performance engine. It often comes equipped with a potent V8 engine that delivers impressive horsepower and torque.
  2. Infotainment System: An advanced infotainment system with a touchscreen display, smartphone integration (such as Apple CarPlay and Android Auto), and various connectivity options.
  3. Performance Modes: Driving modes that allow drivers to customize the car's performance characteristics, including settings for sport, track, and normal driving conditions.
  4. Braking System: High-performance braking system with features like Brembo brakes for enhanced stopping power.
  5. Track-Ready Features: Some Mustang GT models may have track-ready features like performance-oriented tires, aerodynamic enhancements, and cooling systems to handle intense driving conditions.
  6. Transmission Options: Typically available with a manual transmission or an automatic transmission with paddle shifters, allowing drivers to choose their preferred driving experience.
